Experience

From an outsider to technology and development, my journey began in 2023 when I enrolled in Front-End Web Development at Noroff. This pivotal moment sparked a deep passion for front-end development, particularly in UX/UI design. Although I am relatively new to the field, my background as a youth pastor and bodyguard has provided me with extensive experience in handling diverse types of people, enhancing my communication and collaboration skills. My journey in development has primarily been through academic projects, which you can explore further down the page. These projects showcase my growing expertise and dedication to creating user-centric, interactive solutions. I am excited to continue learning and evolving in this dynamic field.
