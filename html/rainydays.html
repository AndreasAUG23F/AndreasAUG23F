Rainy Days

I look back on this project with a smile because it was so much fun to finally start coding. At the same time, I can't help but laugh a little at myself. It's like looking at a drawing you did in elementary school—back when you thought you were really good, only to realize now how much you'd do differently. But that’s the charm of it; everything has a beginning, and this is mine. Rainy Days is a website I created as part of my HTML & CSS course in the front-end development program at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media. The site is designed to sell high-quality outdoor jackets for those who love adventures in the rain. This project was my very first foray into web development using HTML and CSS, and it was an exciting and invaluable learning experience. Even though I would approach many things differently now, Rainy Days stands as a testament to my journey's start. It reminds me of the joy and challenges of those early days, and I’m proud of how far I've come since then.
