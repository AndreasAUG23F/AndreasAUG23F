Peaks & Powder

Reflecting on my final project, "Peaks & Powder," from the spring 2024 semester, I'm reminded of the challenges and growth it brought. This project was a significant milestone for me as it was my first venture into using JavaScript, adding an extra layer of complexity to my development skills. "Peaks & Powder" is an immersive website dedicated to exploring and celebrating the beauty and thrill of mountain sports and skiing. The site offers dynamic features, such as interactive maps, weather forecasts, and user-generated content, providing an engaging experience for outdoor enthusiasts. Creating "Peaks & Powder" was both daunting and exhilarating. It pushed me to expand my programming knowledge and problem-solving abilities, especially when incorporating JavaScript functionalities. Despite the hurdles faced along the way, seeing the project come to life was incredibly rewarding. Looking back, I'm proud of the progress I made during this project and the foundation it laid for my continued growth as a front-end developer. "Peaks & Powder" serves as a reminder of the dedication and passion I have for creating engaging and user-centric web experiences.
