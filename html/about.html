About Me

Hello! My name is Andreas Abrahamsen. I am a dedicated Front-End Developer and Graphic Designer with a passion for creating interactive and user-friendly solutions based in Norway. This website showcases my journey and work in the field of web development and UX/UI design. This website is a work in progress where I will continuously post projects and various work related to development and UX/UI design. Want to know more about who I am, and what I am passionate about? Read more below!

Background

I'm new to web development, coming from a background in security and customer relations. Yet, my passion for UX/UI design and front-end development keeps me learning and evolving. Experienced in understanding diverse user needs, I specialize in crafting visually appealing, functional websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Figma. My journey's just starting, but my dedication to creating engaging user experiences is unwavering.

Personal

When I am not coding or designing, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. Beyond my professional endeavors, I am an enthusiast of martial arts, which often inspires my creative process. Whether it's playing the guitar, or skiing, I find that my hobbies contribute to a well-rounded approach to my work, fostering both innovation and attention to detail. I find inspiration in the world around me and am always looking for new ways to express my creativity. I am excited to continue my journey in web development and UX/UI design and am eager to see where it takes me.
